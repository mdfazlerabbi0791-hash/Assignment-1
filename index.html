Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the ticket?
Ticket includes access to all sessions, workshops, networking events, and refreshments.
Will the sessions be recorded?
Yes, all sessions will be recorded and made available to ticket holders for 30 days after the event.
Can I get a refund?
Full refunds are available up to 7 days before the event. After that, tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred.
Do you provide a certificate?
Yes, all attendees will receive a digital certificate of participation after the event concludes.
Is food included?
Yes, lunch and refreshments are included. We also accommodate dietary restrictions — just let us know in advance.
How can I contact the organizers?
You can reach us at support@event.com or through the contact form on our website. We typically respond within 24 hours.
